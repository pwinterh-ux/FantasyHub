Privacy Policy

Effective: September 12, 2025 · Provider: Kingdom Tools LLC (Dupage County, Illinois)

This Privacy Policy explains how Kingdom Tools LLC (“we,” “us,” “our”) collects, uses, and protects information in connection with RosterDash (the “Service”). By using the Service, you agree to this Policy.

1) What We Collect

  • Account Data: Username, email, and basic profile fields you provide on registration or later.
  • Operational Data from MFL: League IDs/hosts, standings, rosters, future draft picks, pending trades, and similar data fetched via MFL’s APIs using your authorization. We do not store your MFL password.
  • Auth Tokens/Cookies: MFL session cookies (e.g., MFL_USER_ID) or league-scoped API keys needed to make authorized API calls on your behalf. We store these securely and tie them to your account.
  • Usage & Logs: Timestamps, API call metadata, feature usage (e.g., mass-offer counts), error logs. We may store request/response metadata for troubleshooting and abuse prevention.
  • Billing Data: Stripe provides us with non-sensitive billing metadata (e.g., customer ID, plan/price, subscription status). We do not see full card numbers.
  • Device/Technical Data: IP address, user agent, and similar standard web logs for security and analytics.

2) How We Use Information

  • Operate and improve the Service (e.g., show your leagues, submit lineups/trades you ask us to submit, enforce plan limits).
  • Authenticate to MFL on your behalf using your valid session cookie/API key and your league permissions.
  • Detect abuse (rate limits, unusual automation) and comply with MFL’s acceptable use limits.
  • Provide support, send important notices (service changes, legal updates), and process payments via Stripe.
  • Aggregate/anonymous analytics to improve features and performance.

3) Cookies & Similar Technologies

We use our own cookies/sessions to keep you logged in. We store MFL session cookies/token values server-side to make calls on your behalf. Stripe may set cookies on checkout pages. You can block cookies in your browser, but the Service may not function correctly.

4) Sharing

  • Service Providers: Stripe (payments), hosting, logging/monitoring providers. Access is limited to what’s necessary.
  • Legal: We may disclose information if required by law, subpoena, or to protect rights, safety, or integrity of the Service.
  • No Sale of Personal Data: We do not sell your personal information.

5) Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards. No system is 100% secure. You are responsible for safeguarding your account credentials.

6) Data Retention

We retain account and operational data for as long as needed to provide the Service and for legitimate business purposes (e.g., logs, fraud prevention, tax/accounting). Upon account deletion, we’ll delete or de-identify personal data subject to legal/operational limits (e.g., payment records).

7) Your Choices & Rights

  • Update your profile information in the app.
  • Unlink or reauthenticate MFL if your session expires or you want to revoke access.
  • Contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of your data. We may need to verify your identity and may retain certain data as required by law.

8) Children

The Service is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from them.

9) International Users

Our infrastructure may be located in the United States. By using the Service, you consent to processing in the U.S., which may have different data protection laws than your country.

10) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be notified via reasonable means (e.g., in-app notice). Your continued use after changes take effect constitutes acceptance.

11) Contact

Questions? Contact Kingdom Tools LLC via the support channel listed in the app.
