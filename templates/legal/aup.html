Acceptable Use Policy

Effective: September 12, 2025 · Provider: Kingdom Tools LLC (Dupage County, Illinois)

This Acceptable Use Policy (“AUP”) governs how you may use RosterDash (the “Service”). It is part of the Terms of Service. Violations may result in throttling, suspension, or termination.

1) Legal & Ethical Use

  • Do not use the Service to violate any law or third-party rights.
  • No harassment, threats, hate, or abusive conduct.
  • No fraud, impersonation, or deceptive activity.

2) Security & Abuse

  • Do not access accounts, leagues, or data you are not authorized to access.
  • Do not introduce malware or attempt to disrupt the Service or MFL.
  • Do not probe, scan, or test vulnerabilities of our systems or MFL’s systems.

3) Respect for MFL Terms & Limits

  • Use the Service only in ways that comply with MFL terms, policies, and technical limits.
  • No harvesting/scraping of league or user data beyond what is necessary for your permitted use.
  • No actions that overload or degrade MFL or our Service (e.g., high-frequency polling, concurrent mass calls) beyond reasonable limits. We may throttle requests and cache data to comply.

4) Mass Offers & Automation

  • Use mass-offer tools responsibly and only for leagues where you have permission to trade.
  • Do not spam or harass other league members. Respect league rules and etiquette.
  • Do not bypass plan limits or attempt to manipulate counters/quotas.

5) Content

  • Do not post or transmit obscene, pornographic, or otherwise unlawful content through the Service.
  • Do not upload content that infringes copyright, trademark, patent, trade secret, or other rights.

6) Resale & Misrepresentation

  • Do not resell access to the Service without our written permission.
  • Do not misrepresent affiliation with Kingdom Tools LLC or MyFantasyLeague.com.

7) Enforcement

We may log activity, limit or throttle requests, and suspend or terminate access to protect the Service and comply with MFL. Where appropriate, we may contact league commissioners or MFL in cases of suspected abuse or security incidents.

8) Changes

We may update this AUP as needed. Material updates will be communicated by reasonable means (e.g., in-app notice). Continued use after changes take effect constitutes acceptance.

9) Contact

Questions or reports of abuse? Contact Kingdom Tools LLC via the support channel listed in the app.
